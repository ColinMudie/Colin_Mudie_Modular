Mecha King Ghidorah

My First Recording on My Modular System! This Track Really Featured My ALM Akemies Castle making tons of FM synthesis noise as well as my Mutable Instrument's Plaits creating that very ominous bass pound.

My inspiration for this patch was the moment when Mecha King Ghidorah appeared in front of Godzilla.

Flow

My last track was stressful so for my next recording I wanted to showcase Plaits again, but this time with its lush chords. For this patch I'm using the Music Thing Modular that I soldered together to have a random pattern that slightly repeats.

I took the video for this patch at Mill Pond Park in Mt Pleasant, MI.

Hallucination

It had been a while since I got a new module, Modular isn't cheap! This patch was meant to feature my brand new module Monsoon. It's custom built version of Mutable Instruments Clouds that was built for me by Big T Music over in the UK. For this patch, I recorded some of Plaits' chords into the Monsoon then repatched everything with Plaits as the drum voice. Fun rhythms coming from ALM Pamela's New Workout.

No fun video for this patch. I decided to try and make it unique without a video attached to it.
